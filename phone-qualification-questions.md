# F3 Flag — Phone Qualification Questions

These are the **development** and **availability** questions we removed from the online
application to reduce friction (the form is now "apply in ~2 minutes"). Use them to
qualify each applicant on the phone instead. Starred (★) questions are the ones that
were **required** on the old form — treat those as your must-asks.

---

## Development & Fit

The goal here is to gauge **coachability, motivation, and developmental fit** — the
things that protect the standard of the room.

1. ★ **What does your athlete most want to improve?**
2. ★ **Why is your athlete interested in F3?**
3. ★ **How does your athlete typically respond to direct coaching?**
4. What does being a serious athlete mean to your family?
5. Can you give one example of your athlete working to improve *outside* team practice?
6. What are your athlete's current strengths?
7. What areas have coaches identified for improvement?
8. What would make F3 Flag successful for your athlete?
9. Any injuries, physical limitations, allergies, accessibility needs, or medical
   considerations the coaching staff should know about?
   *(Private — coaching staff only.)*

---

## Availability & Scheduling

The goal here is to confirm they can actually **show up consistently** before a spot
is offered.

1. ★ **Can the athlete attend weekly sessions beginning in August 2026?**
   *(Yes / Most weeks / No · unsure)*
2. ★ **Can the athlete attend an assessment / orientation before the program begins?**
   *(Yes / Probably / No · unsure)*
3. ★ **Can the athlete attend weekly sessions consistently?**
   *(Yes / Most weeks / No · unsure)*
4. Preferred session window? *(Morning / Midday / Afternoon / No preference)*
5. Will the athlete participate in another sport during the program? *(Yes / No)*
6. Any known recurring scheduling conflicts?

---

## Quick call flow (suggested)

1. **Open** — confirm name, athlete age/grade, position.
2. **Motivation** — Dev Q1–Q3 (the must-asks). Listen for *wants to improve* vs *wants attention*.
3. **Coachability** — Dev Q3 plus an example (Q5). This is the real filter.
4. **Logistics** — Availability Q1–Q3. If they can't commit weekly, it's not a fit.
5. **Money** — confirm they understand $199/mo founding rate (locked for life; regular $299).
6. **Close** — if it's a fit, send the private enrollment link.

> Reminder of the standard from the site: *we're selecting on commitment, coachability,
> consistency, availability, and fit — not reputation.*
